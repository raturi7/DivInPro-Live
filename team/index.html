Dr JUHI GARG

Founder & Managing Director

Dr Juhi Garg has diverse experience of 20 years in industry and academia and has been a seasoned higher education academician, researcher, and leader in academic administration, entrepreneurship development, ... women empowerment, CSR, and business development. Dr Garg has earned her Doctoral (PhD) degree in the field of Management and holds a Master of Business Administration (MBA) Degree along with an additional Master of Arts (MA) Degree in Psychology along with various certifications in her expert domains. She owns a Copyright from Registrars of Copyright, Copyright Office, New Delhi, Government of India for developing and standardising a Psychometric tools.She has been the principal investigator and consultant to various Government, non—Govt and private projects. She also mentors various startups and are on their advisory board. Dr Juhi is a sought-after speaker and has delivered numerous talks and expert lectures at various Government and private institutions. She is associated with NGOs and startups on a pro bono basis and loves contributing back to society.   She is passionate about sports, music, and writing apart from her zest for creating value through her work.
